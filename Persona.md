For our project, we have created 2 different personas

# Persona 1
## Michael Age: 21
He's a University of Washington student. During his free time, he enjoys kayaking, and fishing to take the stress of his mind.

### Goals: 
Check if it’s safe to swim, paddle, or fishing is safe in the current water area.

Avoid getting sick

Make last-minute decisions easily

### Pain Points: 
Government websites feel overwhelming

Map interfaces are hard to read on mobile

# Persona 2
## Eric Age: 37
He is an Environmental Advocate from the Seattle Area. He already understands wastewater systems and CSOs. He actively participates in shoreline cleanups and water testing events.

### Goals:
Track overflow patterns

Advocate for infrastructure improvements

Share information with his community

### Pain Points: 
No Proactive Notifications

Passive Website Design

Lack of Personal & Community Impact Visibility
