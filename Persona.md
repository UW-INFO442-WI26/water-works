For our project, we have created 2 different personas

# Persona 1
## Michael Age: 21
He's a University of Washington student.
Recreational kayaker and fisher
Lives near campus, and relies heavily on his phone for information

### Background
Michael uses outdoor activities as a stress reliever from school. He often makes plan with friends to kayak or fish along the Seattle's waterfront. He doesn't actively follow wastewater news and only checks safety conditions when he's about to head out.

### Goals: 
Check if it’s safe to swim, kayak, or fishing

Avoid getting sick by exposure to contaiminated water

Make last-minute decisions easily

### Pain Points: 
Government websites feel overwhelming

Map interfaces are hard to read on mobile

# Persona 2
## Eric Age: 37
He is an Environmental Advocate from the Seattle Area. 
Active in shoreline cleanups and water testing events.

### Background:
Eric already understands combined sewer overflows and wastewater infrastructure. He follows local environmental policy and frequently shares information with his network. He is interested not just in whether water is safe today, but in long-term patterns and systemic improvements.

He is comfortable navigating complex tools but prefers platforms that allow deeper engagement and community interaction.

### Goals:
Track CSO frequency and patterns

Advocate for infrastructure improvements

Share information with his community

### Pain Points: 
No Proactive Notifications about CSO events

Passive Website Design

Lack of Personal & Community Impact Visibility
